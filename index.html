About

Heart Failure Decision Support System (DSS), a groundbreaking platform that redefines heart attack prediction through the synergy of cutting-edge machine learning and clinical expertise. Our DSS revolutionizes clinical decision-making by providing healthcare professionals with a dynamic toolkit to predict heart attack risks using an array of machine learning algorithms.

Heart Failure DSS empowers healthcare professionals to enhance patient outcomes through adaptable and personalized risk assessments. By affording clinicians the flexibility to choose predictive algorithms, our system facilitates informed decision-making, enabling effective preventive strategies and interventions.
